# 3 Years

A picture of success across all life areas — what does 3 years from now look like?

## Finances
Stable and self-sufficient. Living within means, savings intact, no financial stress.

## Health
Strong, energetic, capable. Regular exercise is non-negotiable. Body supports all other goals.

## Spirituality
Present and grounded. A daily practice that shapes decisions and perspective.

## Family
Deep connections. Showing up consistently, being present for milestones, honoring those who came before.

## Community & Relationships
Being a reliable neighbor, friend, and participant. Known for showing up when it matters.

## Creativity
Making things regularly. Skills are growing — not perfection, but progression. A body of work exists.

## Outdoor Adventure
Regular time in nature. New places explored. Seasonal activities pursued. Body tested by real challenges.

## Home & Environment
A space that reflects values — simple, functional, peaceful. No clutter, no chaos.

## Productivity & Systems
Systems run on autopilot. Weekly review is a trusted rhythm. Tools serve the life, not the other way around.

## Rest & Recovery
Energy is managed as carefully as time. Rest is scheduled, not stolen. Showing up fully for what matters.
